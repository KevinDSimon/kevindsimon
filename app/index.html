About Me

My name is Kevin Simon, and I am a graphic designer from Chicago, IL. My background was straight print design, before I opened up Dreamweaver CS3 at the age of 17. Ever since then, I have been striving to improve my skills in a variety of mediums. I currently work at Vivid Seats, a secondary ticket marketplace leader, where my time is divided between production and web development.

I have extensive experience in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and Dreamweaver and am very proficient in HTML and CSS.

Design is my passion. It surrounds me always and affects all my decisions. The best part of being a graphic designer is the opportunity to create unique things and share them with others. Most of the time, nobody knows the face behind a design, but it is always rewarding to know that you were a part of the creation.

When I'm not online, I am fishing, golfing, or out with a camera in hand.
